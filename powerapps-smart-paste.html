Revolutionize Data Entry with Copilot’s Form Fill Assistance 

 

Data entry can be time-consuming and prone to errors. The new Copilot Form Fill Assistance feature in model-driven apps addresses this by providing intelligent field value suggestions, complete with citations. This enhancement streamlines the data entry process, ensuring accuracy and saving valuable time. 

 

Accelerate Processes with Smart Paste 

 

Introducing the Smart Paste option—an innovative feature that allows users to populate forms effortlessly. By copying content from any source and utilizing the smart paste function, users receive inline suggestions within the form, expediting the data entry process and reducing manual effort. 

 

Enhanced Form Filling Experience 

 

Beyond text, Copilot’s capabilities now extend to interpreting images, files, and emails to generate form field suggestions. This multifaceted approach ensures that no matter the data source, your forms are populated swiftly and accurately, enhancing overall productivity.

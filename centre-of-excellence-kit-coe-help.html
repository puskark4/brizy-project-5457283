Understanding the Power Platform CoE

A Center of Excellence serves as a centralized function designed to drive innovation and improvement across your organization. It breaks down geographic and organizational silos, bringing together like-minded individuals with similar business goals to share knowledge and success. By establishing a CoE, your organization can align around business objectives rather than individual department metrics, fostering greater creativity and innovation.
